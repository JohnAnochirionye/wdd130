Overview

Purpose

To be the leading company in the white water rafting industry and to provide the first-hand information regarding white water rafting sites, accessiblity, reservation, safety guides and operating devices. The website is needed to provide information regarding the mode of operation associated with rafting exhibitions, The website shall serve the purpose of providing information to our audience or esteem customers and for revenue generation. Our company shall benefit from the website as it welcomes more traffic from our audience, leading to more revenue generation with high media presence

Audience

The expected audience to this website shall be adults captured in different categories or class. The website is built with sportsmen and sportswomen, recreational lovers, and acedemia who engages in outdoor recreational activities. With well informed content, easy site accessbility and navigation links, with well captured pictorial and graphical contents as well as content promoting fonts, color and style, the website shall be appealing to our audience.
