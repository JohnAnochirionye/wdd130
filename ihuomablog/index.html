African Men Fashion World

The traditional attire of the Igbo men includes an Isiagu top which is patterned with lion heads embroidered on their clothing. The Isiagu top is usually worn with trousers and traditional striped hats. The Isiagu, also called Chieftaincy, is a pullover shirt similar to the dashiki that is worn by many African people groups, including the Igbo people
